These javadoc files constitute the “Faces Managed Bean Annotation Specification for Containers Conforming to Jakarta Servlet 2.5 and Beyond”

DEPRECATED The specification for managed beans from Jakarta Faces has been placed into its own specifications, namely Managed Beans and CDI.

The Faces Managed Bean Annotation Specification for Containers will be pruned in a future version of Jakarta Faces, likely Faces 4.0.

The annotations must be processed as specified in section 11.4.1 "Requirements for scanning of classes for annotations" of the Jakarta Faces Specification Document.
